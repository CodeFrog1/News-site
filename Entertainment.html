By Game boy

skills to developing children, adolescents, and adults. Skills such as eye-hand cordination,

Video games nowadays, however, incorporate so much of these elements and more to the point

time such as headaches, memory problems, lack of sleep, and tough times focusing on other activities.

That is why depite the outdatedness and supposed "boringness" of Pong it is the best game to develop
simple skills. Its simplicity leads to better speciialized skill development because of how predicatble and
repedative the game becomes one speciializes in planning, eye-hand cordination and reflexes. Such simple
skill development can lead one to greater oportunities down the road and a foundation for future development.
